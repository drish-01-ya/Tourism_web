Pathanamthitta

Chuttippara

Chuttipara is a Located in Pathanamthitta district of Kerala. This 200 ft. rock cluster is one of the tourist spots where you can get a panoramic view of Pathanamthitta town. Atop the hill, there is a temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, Harihara Mahadeva Temple. Chelavirichapara, Kattadipara and Pulipara are other hills around Chuttipara.

Kottappara Hills Top View Point

Kottappara hills will give you a great trip with natural beauty around. Kottappara is a mountain and a viewpoint located in Idukki district of Kerala. The view from Kottapara hilltop is mind-blowing. The most spectacular view from the peak can be best enjoyed at early morning before 7am. It is a well known picnic spot for locals. It is famous among travelers as Kottapara view point can be accessed easily from Muvattupuzha, Thodupuzha and Kothamangalam. To reach Kottapara take Vannappuram towards the Mullaringad road. You can easily get bus from Vannappuram. Further you can take auto rickshaw till you reach Kottapara peak.

Pandalam Palace

Pandalam dynasty a royal dynasty emerged from a branch of Pandya kingdom.[2] which existed in Kerala during the Kollam era. They came to Kerala fearing the assault of a ruler. In Kerala they were given land and status by Kaipuzha Thampan (Kunjunni Varma Thampan) of Nilambur Kovilakam a landlord who lived in Amanthur Palace at Kaipuzha from Kottayam Kerala. Today Pandalam is part of Pathanamthitta, Kerala, India.
